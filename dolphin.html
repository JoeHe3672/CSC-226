Dolphins

Dolphins are fascinating marine mammals known for their intelligence, playfulness, and social nature. I love dolphins because they embody grace and curiosity, and they seem to have a natural connection with humans.

Fun Facts About Dolphins

  • Dolphins are highly intelligent and can recognize themselves in mirrors.
  • They communicate using clicks, whistles, and body movements.
  • There are over 40 different species of dolphins found worldwide.
